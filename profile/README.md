## Ideation model ‚öñÔ∏è

![Freedom](https://img.shields.io/badge/Freedom-Encouraged-success)
![Responsibility](https://img.shields.io/badge/Responsibility-Required-critical)

### Freedom
- Early-stage ideas can be shared without immediate technical or commercial constraints  
- Experimental, unconventional, and incomplete ideas are welcome  
- Ideas that later prove impractical still create learning value  

### Guidelines
- Use respectful, constructive, and ethical language  
- Do not include personal data, confidential information, or legal risks  
- Whenever possible, describe:
  - the problem  
  - the value proposition  

Our goal is **not to limit creativity**, but to ensure a **productive and sustainable environment**.

![AI](https://img.shields.io/badge/AI-Human%20in%20the%20Loop-informational)

AI-powered tools may be used to support ideation and refinement:

- Structuring raw ideas  
- Exploring alternative perspectives  
- Identifying potential risks and edge cases  
- Improving clarity and documentation quality  

AI outputs are **supportive inputs**, not final decisions.  
Final responsibility always remains human-driven.
