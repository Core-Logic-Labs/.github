## Ideation model âš–ï¸

<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Freedom-Encouraged-success" />
<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Responsibility-Required-critical" />

### Freedom
- Early-stage ideas can be shared without immediate technical or commercial constraints  
- Experimental, unconventional, and incomplete ideas are welcome  
- Ideas that later prove impractical still generate learning value  

### Guidelines
- Use respectful, constructive, and ethical language  
- Do not include personal data, confidential information, or legally sensitive content  
- Whenever possible, clearly describe:
  - the problem  
  - the value proposition  

Our goal is not to limit creativity, but to ensure a **productive and sustainable environment**.

<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/AI-Human%20in%20the%20Loop-informational" />

AI-powered tools may be used to support ideation and refinement processes, including:
- Structuring raw ideas  
- Exploring alternative perspectives  
- Identifying potential risks and edge cases  
- Improving clarity and documentation quality  

AI outputs are **supportive inputs**, not final decisions.  
Final evaluation and accountability always remain human-driven.
