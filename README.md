# IDEA Repo

This repository is created to define our organization’s approach to idea generation, creative culture, and working principles.

The content here explains how ideas related to the organization are produced, evaluated, and documented, as well as the boundaries and freedoms within the idea generation process.

This repository serves as the organization’s official idea pool.

---

## Organizational Purpose

The core purpose of our organization is to encourage problem-oriented thinking, generate innovative and sustainable ideas, and gradually transform those ideas into concrete projects.

Within this scope:
- New product and service ideas are developed  
- Improvements for existing processes and systems are proposed  
- Emerging technologies and new approaches are continuously evaluated  

Idea generation is a fundamental value of our organization.

---

## Role of This Repository

This repository:
- Acts as the central space where organizational ideas are formally documented  
- Enables tracking ideas from early concepts to more mature stages  
- Establishes a shared language and approach for idea development  

This is not a code repository, but a repository of ideas and methodologies.

---

## Freedom and Guidelines in Idea Generation

Idea generation within our organization is conducted within a framework of conscious freedom.

### Freedom Areas
- Early-stage ideas can be shared without immediate technical or commercial constraints  
- Experimental, unconventional, or immature ideas are encouraged  
- Ideas that may later prove impractical are still considered valuable  

### Core Guidelines
- Ideas must be expressed in a respectful, constructive, and ethical manner  
- Content involving personal data, confidential information, or legal risks must not be shared  
- Each idea should, where possible, be framed around a problem and a value proposition
